# Galaxy Wireless Connection Instructions ## Wireless Bluetooth connection ### Turn on BT / Pairing BT - Turn on Wireless mode - Press [Fn]+[Q]/[W]/[E] to switch to BT mode (for the associated memory slot) - Long press [Fn]+[Q]/[W]/[E] to turn on pairing to the BT memory slot ## 2.4G wireless connection ### Turn on 2.4GHz / Pairing 2.4GHz - Turn on the wireless mode - Press [Fn]+[R] to switch to 2.4GHz mode - Long press [Fn]+[R] to enter 2.4GHz pairing mode ## Turning off Either: - Turn off the keyboard - After 30 seconds of not being able to find the connected device the keyboard turns off automatically ## Function Key Options |Combination buttons|WIN function|MAC function (single press trigger)|Describe| |---|---|---|---| |[Fn]+[F1]|This Computer|Screen Brightness -| | |[Fn]+[F2]|Homepage|Screen brghtness +| | |[Fn]+[F3]|Calculator|Multi Task View| | |[Fn]+[F4]|Mailbox|Program List| | |[Fn]+[F5]|Media Player|n/a| | |[Fn]+[F6]|Stop|n/a| | |[Fn]+[F7]|Previous Song|Previous Song| | |[Fn]+[F8]|Play/Pause|Play/Pause| | |[Fn]+[F9]|Next Song|Next Song| | |[Fn]+[F10]|Mute|Must| | |[Fn]+[F11]|Volume +|Volume +| | |[Fn]+[F12]|Volume -|Volume -| | |[Fn]+[Backspace]|Reset (long press)|Reset (long press)| Backlight flasses white 3x when done| |[Fn]+[F\]|Switch Backlight Colour|Switch Backlight Colour| Order: Colorful, Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Green, Blue, Purple, White | |[Fn]+[Enter]|Backlight Effect|Backlight Effect| 18 Lighting effects available | |[Fn]+[Right Shift]|Indicator Effect|Indicator Effect| Lightwave, Running Horse, Off, Constant, Brightness, Breathing, Neon, Default Light Wave | |[Fn]+[Space Bar]|Keyboard Battery Indicator|Keyboard Battery Indicator| Backlight for keys 1 to 0 lights up to show battery life | |[Fn]+[T]|Backlight On/Off|Keyboard Backlight On/Off| | |[Fn]+[Win]+[L]|Lock Win Key|n/a| | |[Fn]+[Win]+[<-]|Light Effect Speed -
